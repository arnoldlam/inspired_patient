Our Story

The result of a real world need to better manage the health journey. Inspired Patient was born.

Many patients and caregivers today are challenged by the health care journey. And professionals face similar challenges in engaging and inspiring patients to the best health outcome possible.

Inspired Patient solves this big problem by providing a web platform for improving the patient's health journey. We solve this problem by providing information, organization and collaboration tools to better managed the health journey.

Inspired Patient. Improving the health journey by empowering you to manage your health.

The Team

Learn more about the Inspired Patient Team.

Dan DeBeyer

Founder

Dan has spent a 20 year career in Life Sciences Product Development and Commercialization. His inspiration for Inspired Patient was his family's health journey of the past 15 years.

Dan volunteers extensively including mentoring health students at the University of British Columbia.

Fred Rego

Advisor

Fred Rego has been involved with technology startups since he was 18 years old. He started his career in Rio de Janeiro Brazil and worked at Accenture and 3 software development start-ups before moving to Vancouver, Canada to work at a Health Care software company.

He has a Bachelor of Science degree from Rio de Janeiro’s Federal University (Brazil) and has attended the University of British Columbia’s Sauder Business School extension program.

Dr. Stephen Tredwell, MD Professor Emeritus, Orthopaedics, UBC

Advisor

Dr. Tredwell retired from clinical practice in 2006 as Department Head, Paediatric Orthopaedics, BC Children's Hospital. His ongoing interests include medical ethics and areas related to patient autonomy and the patient/doctor partnership.

Dr. Tredwell earned his MD at UBC and a MA in Organizational Change and Conflict Management from Royal Roads University.

Sandra Bessler

Advisor
